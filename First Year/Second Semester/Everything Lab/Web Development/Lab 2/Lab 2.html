Bake a Cake

  1. Mix the ingredients.
  2. Prepare the baking pan.
  3. Bake the cake.
    • Preheat the oven.
    • Place the batter in the pan.
    • Set the timer and bake for 30 minutes.
  4. Cool the cake before serving
